About ParkEasy

ParkEasy is an innovative web platform designed to address the growing issue of parking scarcity in urban neighborhoods. Our mission is to optimize space utilization by allowing residents to rent out their unused parking spaces to drivers, providing a convenient solution for both space owners and renters.

Our Mission

We aim to reduce congestion and the environmental impact caused by drivers circling streets in search of parking. By turning unused parking spaces into a source of income for residents and a solution for drivers, ParkEasy enhances urban mobility and improves the quality of life in crowded neighborhoods.

How It Works

ParkEasy utilizes a straightforward tech stack of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a SQL-based database to offer a user-friendly interface, secure transactions, and scalability. Our platform displays real-time availability of parking spots, allowing users to easily find and book the perfect space.

Our Team

Our team is dedicated to improving urban mobility through innovative technology. We work tirelessly to ensure ParkEasy provides a seamless experience for both parking space owners and renters.
